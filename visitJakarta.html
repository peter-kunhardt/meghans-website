Visit Jakarta

Street food, restaurants and coffee shops

Jarakta offers an incredible assortment of street food but when you are ready to sit down, the world famous Koko Bogana offers traditional delacies you won't find anywhere else! Giyanti Coffee Roastery, and it's neighbor Koultoura Coffee, have coffees from all over the world. Perfect for anyone with a palet that likes to explore!

At sunset treat yourself to fine dining at one of these award-winning restaurants. The service, ambiance, and chef creations are sure to impress:

  • Tugu Kuntskring Paleis
  • Namaaz Dining
  • Amuz
  • Animale

Places to visit

You won't get bored when you visit Jakarta. This vibrant city has so much to offer. It's well known for its vibrant street life which is juxtaposed with quiet, meditative spaces for you to contemplate your next move.

It might be your first visit, but it won't be your last! Come of the excitment, stay for the relaxation! Here are a few places for your bucket-list!

  • Merdeka Palace. The official residence of The President
  • Senayan City Shopping Mall. For upscale department stores and the trendiest botiquest
  • Kota Tua. You'll love the Dutch-inspired architecture
  • Pasar Seni Ancol. Live performers and art market.
  • Kidzania Pacific Palace. Indonesia's famous theme park.
